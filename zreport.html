How to Use Z Report
Submitting a Z Report

Tap a date on the calendar — green means done, red means missing. Fill in Sales, Tax, Credit Card, and Cash from your Z tape at closing. Pick the employee and tap Submit.

Correcting an Entry

Tap a green (already submitted) date. The form pre-fills with existing data and the button changes to "Update." Edit and resubmit to overwrite.

Missing Entries Banner

The red chips below the calendar show dates with no entry in the last 60 days. Tap a chip to jump directly to that date.

Employee Access

Employees tap "Other Store," select your store, choose "Employee," and enter the employee PIN. They only see the Submit tab. Share the employee PIN from Settings → Employee PIN.

Gateway Status (Admin)

The pill in the top-right shows the Google Sheets sync status. Green = live. Red = offline. If offline, check your internet and try the Sync button on the This Month tab.

Your Data Is Stored Locally

All Z reports are saved on this device — no internet required after setup. Use the Export buttons in Data Management below to back up your records or move them to another device.
