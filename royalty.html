How to Use
Monthly Process — 8 Steps
1
Fill in the Stamps tab using your 4×6 tally card (sales log) and physical count (ending inventory). Enter beginning inventory from last month's ending.
2
Fill in the Metered Mail tab using Stamps.com Balances report and Endicia Appendix A for the exclusion amount.
3
Enter values in Center Management → Stamps & Metered Mail using the summary values from tabs 1–2. Click Save before leaving.
4
Fill in the Products tab using FRS Profit Center Report (Discounted Income column). Stamp Sales auto-fills from the Stamps tab.
5
Review the Exclusions tab — verify no red warnings. Enter iShip fee. Leave Other 1 at $0 unless area rep instructs.
6
Review the Commissions tab — leave all at $0 unless area rep instructs otherwise.
7
Review the Summary tab — record Total Due. Complete the checklist. Submit in Center Management and enter the confirmation number.
8
Hit Reset / New Month when done. Stamp pricing is preserved automatically.

Key Rules
Always use USPS cost basis for inventory entries Stamps Purchases and Ending Stamps Inventory must use what the STORE paid USPS — not what the customer paid. Mixing cost and retail is the #1 cause of Stamp Cost exclusion errors.
Submission deadlineSubmit by the 5th of the following month. Center Management requires Google Chrome only.
QuickBooks noteStamp cost/inventory journals must be done manually in QuickBooks — they are not included in the QuickBooks import file.
PRP commissionsPre-populated by HQ in Other 1 (Commissions tab in CM). Do not enter them in your POS system or in Other 2.
