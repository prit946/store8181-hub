How to Use
Monthly Process — 3 Steps
1
Throughout month — keep the 4×6 Stamp Activity Card handy. Front: log each sale (Date · B or C · Qty). Back: log each USPS order as it arrives (Date · B or C · Qty · Total Cost). At month end, open Stamps.com → Reports → Balances → Summary → select the month. Copy the 4 dollar amounts to the back of the card (Beginning, Funds Added, Funds Deducted, Ending — skip Funds Credited, it's always $0).
2
Royalty time — open this tool and type in your card. Stamps tab: beginning inventory, purchases, daily sales, ending count. Metered tab: the 4 Stamps.com dollar amounts. Exclusion: only enter an amount if you printed USPS labels through the meter this month (run Endicia Appendix A report) — otherwise leave $0. CM Guide tab shows exact values to enter in CM.
3
Submit in CM — Center Management → My Center → Royalty → Submit Royalty → select month → Stamps and Metered Mail. Enter each value from the CM Guide tab and click Save. Exclusions tab: iShip auto-fills from POS (verify it), Other 1 Adjustment is almost always $0. Commissions tab: Other 1 (PRP) is pre-filled by HQ, Other 2 Adjustment is almost always $0. Products/Services tab auto-populates from POS — leave it. View & Submit Royalty: review and submit.

Rules to Know
Stamp Cost ≤ Stamp Sales — always use USPS cost (what the store paid) for inventory, never retail. Mixing cost and retail is the #1 cause of CM submission blocks.
Submission deadline — 5th of the following month. Center Management requires Google Chrome only.
Commissions tab — Other 1 (PRP) and Other 2 are both pre-populated by HQ from POS. Only "Other 2 Adjustment" column is editable — leave it at $0.00 unless your area rep instructs a change. Do not enter commission amounts manually.
