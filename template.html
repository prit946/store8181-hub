Getting Started
1. Set your PINs
Below in PIN Settings — choose a 4-digit Owner PIN and Employee PIN.

2. Fill in your credentials
Go to the Credentials tab — tap any title, username, or password field to edit. QR codes generate automatically.

3. Add or remove profiles
Owner login only — tap + Add Account to create new profiles, or X to delete ones you don't need.

4. Share with employees
Send them this same URL. They log in with the Employee PIN and only see the profiles you marked as employee-visible.

5. Back up your data
Settings → Backup Now — copies all credentials as text. Paste into Apple Notes or Google Docs to keep a safe copy.

6. Changing passwords (every 30 days)
Log in as Owner → tap the password field → type the new password → QR updates instantly.
Then go to Settings → Backup Now → Copy and paste into your Notes app. This is your only copy — do it every time passwords change.

Important: This tool does not auto-sync between devices. When passwords change, notify your employees directly and have them tap the password field and type in the new one. Their QR code updates the moment they do.
